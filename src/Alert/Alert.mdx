# Alerts

## Import

`import { Alert } from 'fannypack'`

## Basic Usage

<Playground>
  <Alert>Anim pariatur cliche reprehenderit, enim eiusmod high life accusamus terry richardson ad squid. Nihil anim keffiyeh helvetica.</Alert>
</Playground>

## Titles

You can set a title with the `title` prop.

<Playground>
  <Alert title="Alert Title">Anim pariatur cliche reprehenderit, enim eiusmod high life accusamus terry richardson ad squid. Nihil anim keffiyeh helvetica.</Alert>
  <Alert title="Alert Title" marginTop="xsmall" />
</Playground>

## Types

An alert can take a type of `"info"`, `"success"`, `"warning"` or `"danger"`.

<Playground>
  <Alert title="Alert Title" type="info" marginTop="xsmall">Anim pariatur cliche reprehenderit, enim eiusmod high life accusamus terry richardson ad squid. Nihil anim keffiyeh helvetica.</Alert>
  <Alert title="Alert Title" type="success" marginTop="xsmall">Anim pariatur cliche reprehenderit, enim eiusmod high life accusamus terry richardson ad squid. Nihil anim keffiyeh helvetica.</Alert>
  <Alert title="Alert Title" type="warning" marginTop="xsmall">Anim pariatur cliche reprehenderit, enim eiusmod high life accusamus terry richardson ad squid. Nihil anim keffiyeh helvetica.</Alert>
  <Alert title="Alert Title" type="danger" marginTop="xsmall">Anim pariatur cliche reprehenderit, enim eiusmod high life accusamus terry richardson ad squid. Nihil anim keffiyeh helvetica.</Alert>
</Playground>

## Icons

You can add a icon to your alert by using the `hasIcon` prop. The icon will default to the status' icon. If you want to change this icon, then configure the `iconNames` attribute in the [Icon theme](/components/icon#theming).

<Playground>
  <Alert title="Alert Title" hasIcon type="info" marginTop="xsmall">Anim pariatur cliche reprehenderit, enim eiusmod high life accusamus terry richardson ad squid. Nihil anim keffiyeh helvetica.</Alert>
  <Alert title="Alert Title" hasIcon type="success" marginTop="xsmall">Anim pariatur cliche reprehenderit, enim eiusmod high life accusamus terry richardson ad squid. Nihil anim keffiyeh helvetica.</Alert>
  <Alert title="Alert Title" hasIcon type="warning" marginTop="xsmall">Anim pariatur cliche reprehenderit, enim eiusmod high life accusamus terry richardson ad squid. Nihil anim keffiyeh helvetica.</Alert>
  <Alert title="Alert Title" hasIcon type="danger" marginTop="xsmall">Anim pariatur cliche reprehenderit, enim eiusmod high life accusamus terry richardson ad squid. Nihil anim keffiyeh helvetica.</Alert>
</Playground>

## Inline alerts

To inline an alert, set the `display` prop to `inline-block`.

<Playground>
  <Alert display="inline-block">This is an inline alert.</Alert>
</Playground>
